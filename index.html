Project Background

The Logic Gap

Discrete mathematics and Boolean logic are notoriously intimidating. Traditional learning methods rely on abstract symbols and rigid notation, making concepts like inference rules, logical operators, and proof construction difficult to grasp.

The Fusion Solution

We transform complex concepts into intuitive energy synthesis mechanics. Players reconstruct logical premises by combining molecular compounds in a high-tech reactor. This creates a low-pressure environment to practice discrete math while synthesizing energy for demanding AI systems.
